TIPS FOR APPLICANTS

针对国际留学生的特别建议 / Tips for International Students

🌐

多语种排版

如果有条件，建议在文案下方附上一段简短的英文版Summary，展示项目的国际化调性

🏠

远程可能性

留学生可能存在时差或签证问题，若能接受部分时间Remote（远程），请在文中注明，这会极大提高投递率

🛠️

强调AI工具链

留学生通常对海外工具（如Notion, Discord, Linear等）更熟悉，可以提到公司使用的协同办公栈，吸引同类属性的人才

🌐

Multilingual Layout

If possible, include a brief English Summary at the bottom of your application to showcase the project's international tone

🏠

Remote Work Options

If you can work remotely part-time due to time zone or visa issues, please mention this — it will greatly increase your response rate!

🛠️

AI Tool Stack

International students are often more familiar with overseas tools (like Notion, Discord, Linear). Feel free to mention your experience with these tools!
